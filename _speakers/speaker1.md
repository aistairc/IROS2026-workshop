Dr. Rika Antonova is an Associate Professor at the University of Cambridge, leading the Cambridge Resilient Autonomous Learning (CamRAL) Lab. Previously, she was at Stanford University upon receiving the NSF/CRA Computing Innovation Fellowship, and worked with the Interactive Perception and Robot Learning Lab at Stanford, led by Jeannette Bohg. She completed her PhD work on data-efficient simulation-to-reality transfer at KTH (Sweden) in the group headed by Danica Kragic. She also had the opportunity to intern at NVIDIA Robotics (Seattle, USA) and Microsoft Research (Cambridge, UK).
